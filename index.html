Welcome to Delta Child

Producer • DJ • AV Artist

Biography

Delta Child is an experimental music producer and DJ based in Sacramento, California. Her sound lives at the intersection of heavy trap drums, glitchy textures, and emotionally tense string arrangements—creating sonic environments that feel as cinematic as they are chaotic.

Producing for over five years and DJing for eight, Delta Child brings a deep sense of rhythm and raw emotion to her sets. Her performances often explore polyrhythmic structures and off-kilter grooves.

She is a visual innovator—using TouchDesigner to craft immersive audiovisual sets where reactive visuals pulse and distort in sync with her sound.

Delta Child contributes editorial work to The Synapse in partnership with Neurodot Studio, exploring the impact of technology on underground music.
